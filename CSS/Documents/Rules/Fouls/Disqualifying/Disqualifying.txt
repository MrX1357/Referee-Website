unsportsmanlike action not only by
players, but also substitutes, coaches,
excluded players or accompanying
delegation members.
Any of the above persons who are
guilty of flagrant acts of aggression
against opponents or officials shall be
disqualified and the crew chief must
report the incident to the organizing
body of the competition.
