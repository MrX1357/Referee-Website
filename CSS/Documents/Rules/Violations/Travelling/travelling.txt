“Travelling is the illegal movement of one foot or both feet
beyond the limits outlined in this article, in any direction,
while holding a live ball on the playing court”

Players on the court move very
dynamically, especially when they
have the ball. Taking control of a ball
or commencing a dribble can occur by
a player from a stationary position or
whilst moving.
