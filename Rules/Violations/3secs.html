RULES PAGE

Rule

3 Seconds

A player shall not remain in the opponent's restricted area for more than 3 consecutive seconds while his/her team is in control of a live ball in the frontcourt and the game clock is running. Referees are not to call a 3 second violation once a shot attempt has been released.

To establish himself/herself outside the restricted area, the player must place both feet on the floor outside the restricted area, but not across the baseline.

Signal

Extra Notes

Referees shall have in mind that there are allowances when a player who is in the restricted area:

  • Makes an attempt to leave it
  • Has one of his team-mates in the act of shooting
  • Dribbles to shoot for a field goal after having been there for less than 3 consecutive seconds

    • Referees need to use voice to prevent the player staying inside the restricted area. Before making a 3 second violation call.
